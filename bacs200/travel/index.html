Adventure Ridge

Take both the summer and winter seasons to new heights. Adventure ridge offers seasonal activites for anyone of any age! From tubing and ski-biking in the winter, to Vail's award winning rope's course, alipine slide, and nature discovery in the summer, you are guranteed to find your best adventure here.

Solaris Square

One of Vail Villages newest editions, Solaris Square, provides a cozy relaxed feel within its walls. Take a spin on the seasonal ice rink in the winter, or a community yoga class on the seasonal turf. Grab your bowling shoes and take a strik at Bol, or grab a hot chocolate and sit back at Yet's Grind, a local delicacy.

Piney Lake

A local secret, piney lake, is a serene lakeside getaway located just 15 short minutes outside of Vail Village. Rent a cabin and have a true Colorado Campout, or take a relaxing day trip and try on of the many hiking trails. Canoes are also available for rent as is fishing gear as well.
