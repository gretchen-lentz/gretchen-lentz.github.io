Welcome to the Skating Club of Vail

For 43 years, SCV has served the Vail Valley providing coaching, ice-time, and a positive figure skating experience for people of all ages. It doesn’t matter if you’re an aspiring Olympian, Disney-On-Ice’s next soloist, or here for the love of the sport, Skating Club of Vail has opportunities for everyone!

SCV's Misson

TO CREATE, MAINTAIN, AND ENHANCE A HEALTHY AND POSITIVE ENVIRONMENT. SO OUR MEMBERS CAN DEVELOP PHYSICALLY AND EMOTIONALLY WHILE BUILDING CHARACTER THROUGH THE SPORT OF FIGURE SKATING.
